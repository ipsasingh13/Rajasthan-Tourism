Sacred Temples & Shrines

Discover the spiritual epicenters of Rajasthan holding profound heritage value.

Khatu Shyamji Dham

A deeply revered shrine in Sikar, Rajasthan. Dedicated to Barbarika, millions visit annually to receive blessings from Baba Shyam in a setting of vibrant architectural faith.

Salasar Balaji Dham

Located in Churu, this holy temple features a unique manifestation of Lord Hanuman with a beard and mustache, serving as a pillar of immense cultural devotion.
